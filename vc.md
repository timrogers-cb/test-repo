## Tips for Approaching Investors

1. **Build in public** — Share progress on GitHub, Twitter/X, and dev communities to demonstrate traction.
2. **Warm introductions** — Use LinkedIn and mutual connections to get introductions to investors rather than cold outreach.
3. **Nail the demo** — A live, working demo is worth more than any pitch deck for a technical product.
4. **Show metrics** — GitHub stars, npm downloads, active users, and revenue are all signals investors look for.
5. **Apply to accelerators early** — Programs like YC and Techstars open doors to investors even after the program ends.
6. **Target investor thesis fit** — Research each firm's portfolio and stated thesis before applying to maximize the chance of a response.
